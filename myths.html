Jean Vanier CSS's Hackclub :)

Here's a few myths we want to bust:

  • We're not malicious. We create applications and learn how to keep them safe by doing CTF-Capture The Flag competitions. In these competitions, we are given permission to try and find a flag inside a server, website, or application. It's a Hands-On way of learning how to make safe programs


  • You don't need to know how to code! We need
    • Artists- websites have to look pretty!
    • Leaders- people with 3A or B lunch that help us set up every meeting and have fun with it!
    • Passionate people- we want people who make the club a fun place to be! It's not a competition all the time!

  • You don't have to come to every meeting!- We're like a weekly hackathon!Every meeting, you come by, we do a quick demo, and we set you off to create a spin off of the demo and then share it with everyone. This can include a cool way to solve a CTF problem, sharing an outside project you've been working on, etc! We're here as a community!
